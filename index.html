Our Story

Welcome to Padaria, a cornerstone of Lisbon's most historic neighborhood since 1999.

Founded by Weihang Hu, our bakery began with a single wood-fired oven and a secret family recipe for pão caseiro. For over three generations, we have risen before the sun to serve our neighbors with a warm smile.

Our Mission

To be a warm, welcoming place for our community, preserving the authentic tastes of Portuguese baking while creating sweet memories for a new generation.

Meet the Family

Today, the bakery is run by Sofia and Tiago, Avó Isabel's grandchildren. They continue to honor her legacy, blending her traditional recipes with a fresh perspective.
